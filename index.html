ABOUT C-Horse Games

At first, working together on mobile games was just a vague idea, but in 2021 we gradually became obsessed with it. We took a year to learn the main syntax of C#, Unity Engine, Adobe Products, Data Analytics and every day we continue growing in those areas. We have also built trustful connections in the industry and working with amazing people on a day-to-day basis.

We are just at the beginning of our journey but we hope you will enjoy our games. Despite a lot left to learn, we can truly guarantee that we invest a lot of personality and love in them.

We hope to share fun and good vibes with you, and from the bottom of our hearts - thank you for choosing CHorse Games!

HIRING PROCESS
