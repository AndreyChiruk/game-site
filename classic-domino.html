Dominoes is definitely the most popular puzzle game throughout the world. It is simple and, at the same time, challenging.

If you're in the mood to play Dominoes, this is a fantastic app to begin with. You will enjoy addictive gameplay while improving your mental and calculative skills. Three domino game modes are designed to give you an interactive and variable gameplay:

1. Classic Dominoes: to win in a round be the first to lay down all of your dominoes. Your score is based on the value of the dominoes left in the opponent's hand.

2. Block Domino: similar to Classic Dominoes, but you have to pass your turn if you are stuck.

3. All Five: you score points by being the first to play all your dominoes or by making the open ends add up to a multiple of five.

This Dominoes game offers you an easy-to-use interface with an opportunity to choose your own backgrounds and domino tile sets!

There are at least 20 different ways to master the game of Dominoes. The more tips and tricks you know, the better your chances of winning.

Features:

  • - Completely free Domino game!
  • - Three Games: Draw Dominoes, Block Dominos and All Fives
  • - Dark and light themes
  • - Tableau and domino tiles customization
  • - Play offline - no Internet connection required
  • - Optimized both for tablets and smartphones
  • - Intuitive interface

We wish you the very best of luck!
